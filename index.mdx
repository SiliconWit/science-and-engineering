**Critical Examination**:
- Conceptual analysis and definition
- Logical argumentation and reasoning
- Historical and comparative perspectives
- Integration of multiple viewpoints

**Ethical Frameworks**:
- Consequentialist and deontological approaches
- Virtue ethics and character-based analysis
- Care ethics and relational perspectives
- Pragmatic and contextual considerations

### Empirical Investigation

**Case Study Analysis**:
- Detailed examination of specific technologies
- Historical development and context
- Stakeholder analysis and impact assessment
- Lessons learned and best practices

**Quantitative Analysis**:
- Statistical analysis of trends and patterns
- Economic impact assessment
- Environmental impact measurement
- Social and behavioral data analysis

- Multiple formats and presentation styles
